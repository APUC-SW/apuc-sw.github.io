# Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy explains how this website (hereinafter referred to as the "Site") handles visitor information. As this Site is a static site utilizing GitHub Pages, the APUC Software site administrator (hereinafter referred to as the "Operator") adheres to the principle of not directly operating a server database or collecting sensitive personal information.

## 1. Items and Methods of Personal Information Collected
1. **Automatically Collected Information**: When visiting the site, visitors' IP addresses, access times, browser information, etc., may be automatically recorded through GitHub server logs.
2. **Third-Party Analytics Tools**: This site may use Google Analytics to improve services, and in this process, anonymized visitor statistics data is collected through cookies.
3. **Directly Provided by Users**: Information directly entered by users (nickname, email address, etc.) may be collected when using sponsorship services, sending chat messages, writing comments, or sending inquiry emails.

## 2. Integration with Third-Party Services and Data Transfer
This site utilizes the following external platforms to provide smooth services, and data may be transferred to the relevant companies when using each service.

| Service Classification | Provider | Related Content |
|---|---|---|
| Hosting | GitHub, Inc. | Site operation infrastructure and visit log management |
| Donation Integration | Buy Me a Coffee | Donation payment processing and donor information management |
| Community | Discord Inc. | Community/Forum Provided |
| Visitor Analytics | Google LLC | Visitor statistics and behavior pattern analysis |

**Important**: All sponsorship payment information (card numbers, account details, etc.) is processed directly by the respective sponsorship platform. The operator of this site never collects or accesses users' sensitive financial information.

## 3. Purpose of Use and Retention Period of Personal Information
1. **Purpose of Use**: Checking content usage statistics, verifying support for creative activities (sponsorship), responding to user inquiries, and improving services.
2. **Retention Period**: As this site does not operate a separate database, it does not directly retain information. Information collected through third-party services is retained for the period stipulated by each service provider's operational policy and relevant laws.

## 4. User Rights and Cookie Settings
Users may exercise the following rights at any time to protect their personal information:
1. **Reject Cookies**: You can refuse or delete the collection of cookies through your web browser settings.
2. **Edit/Delete Information**: Information registered on third-party services (sponsorship platforms, comments, etc.) must be managed directly through the settings of the respective service.
